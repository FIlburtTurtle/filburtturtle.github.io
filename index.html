Hi I'm Rafael Ancheta a freelance web designer and developer based in the Bay Area. I strive for clean contemporary design along with efficient layouts that serve all individuals . I've been working in the field for a year with experience in UI/UX, eccommerce, website design, content management systems, and web app development. While a majority of my experience come from working as a designer for the startup Wavespot, I have also worked with other businesses on smaller scale projects. Please see my portfolio page for more on my past projects as well as a preview on some of my own works in progress.

wavespot.net

Created for the company Wavespot, I designed this site on Wordpress to accompany the clean feeling the CEO was trying to strive for in his brand image. By creating a significant amount of graphics on the site and laying it out from premade content, I created a wavespot.net to appeal to the small business owner.

iCheckBooks

A still in progress work, this app cross references the entirety of english literature for a single purpose to creating and tracking books lists. Users are able to create their own lists, follow the lists of others, and see an aggregate of the most talked about books online.

theloungersclub.com

One of my first projects that kick started my web design and development freelancing. The client wanted a cheap and dirty website that could be completed ASAP. I delivered with theloungersclub.com a simple website that lists the few products that he sells using Paypal as the payment gateway rather than a complex CMS
